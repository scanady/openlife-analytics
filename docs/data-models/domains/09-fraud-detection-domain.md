# Fraud Detection Domain
## Logical Data Model - Entity Definitions

**Domain:** Fraud Detection  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** December 6, 2025  
**Status:** Phase 1 - Entity Identification  
**Phase:** Supporting & Analytical Domain

---

## Domain Overview

**Purpose:** Identify, investigate, and prevent fraudulent activity across the customer lifecycle, including application fraud, claims fraud, and premium fraud.

**Scope:** This domain encompasses fraud alerts, investigations, fraud rules, pattern detection, third-party fraud data, and fraud case management.

**Business Value:**
- Fraud loss prevention and reduction
- Investigation efficiency and effectiveness
- False positive management
- Fraud pattern identification
- Regulatory compliance for fraud reporting
- Risk mitigation across insurance operations
